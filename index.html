Biography

Hello there and thank you for visiting my website!

I'm a third year M.Eng. student at Xi'an Jiaotong University, guided by Prof. Ming Fan and Prof. Ting Liu. I received my B.Eng. from Xi'an Jiaotong University and Engineer's degree from Paris-Saclay University (CentraleSupélec). I'm looking forward to graduating in June 2024. My research is rooted in semantic parsing and natural language processing. I've had the chance to delve into projects on natural language interfaces for database querying and visualization. More recently, I'm focusing on the robustness, trustworthiness, and reliability of large language models. Previously, I have done research internships at Tencent's WeChat Group, Huawei's 2012 Lab, and IDEA Lab. I've also had the pleasure of contributing to research projects at the University of Cambridge and Imperial College London.

As I look toward the future, I am actively seeking Ph.D. positions starting in 2024. If you're interested in NLP, large language models, or see potential for collaborative research, I would be thrilled to connect and discuss further.
